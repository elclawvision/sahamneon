# 🐋 Saham Ultimate: Dev & Marketing Manifesto

**Tagline: "Whale Tracking, Simplified. Data Real-Time, Tanpa Noise."**

## 🎯 Our Mission
Saham Ultimate is designed to bridge the gap between complex KSEI data and actionable trading decisions. We provide a **Premium, High-Density, and Clean** experience for retail traders who want to follow the "Big Money" without getting lost in spreadsheets.

---

## 🚀 Key Features (The "Power" Stack)

### 1. 📈 Multi-Dimensional Ticker Analysis
- **Real-Time KSEI Reporting**: Live data sync as of March 2026.
- **Top Holder Transparency**: Instantly see who owns the most of any stock.
- **Local vs Foreign Mix**: Beautifully calculated percentages to see who is driving the price.

### 2. 🐋 The Investor Tab (Smart Money Tracker)
- **Whale Portfolio View**: Click any investor name to see their *entire* holdings across all tickers.
- **Nationality & Type Tracking**: Identify if a whale is a Local individual, Foreign Institution, or a "Nominee" account.

### 3. 🏢 Conglomerate & Figures Watchlist
- **Group Tracking**: One-click filters for major groups (Salim, Astra, Prajogo, etc.).
- **Finance Celebrities**: Track the moves of influential public figures and "hot" market entities.

### 4. 📊 Free Float Screener (Risk Management)
- **Low Float Detection**: Spot "Saham Gorengan" early with our <15% and <5% filters.
- **Total Held vs Free**: Accurate calculation of the "real" shares available in the market.

## 💎 Our Competitive Advantages (Why We Win)

### ✅ Hard-Core Simplicity (Mobile Friendly)
- **No Manual Entry**: Everything is automated and pre-calculated.
- **Finger-Friendly UI**: Designed for mobile execution. Navigation is solid, fixed, and fast.
- **Zero Lag**: Ultra-fast HMR and optimized data loading.

### ✅ Data Portability (CSV Download)
- **One-Click Export**: Users can export any filtered view to CSV for further Excel analysis. 
- **Teaser Hooks**: Our demo mode shows the "Download" button to prove the feature exists, creating a strong "Need to Login" trigger.

### ✅ Premium Aesthetics
- **Dual Theme Engine**: Fast switching between High-Contrast Dark Mode and Clean Light Mode.
- **No Noise**: We don't show news or fluff. We show **Positions**.

## 📱 Mobile Experience
- **Solid Bottom Nav**: Navigation never jumps. It stays where your thumb is.
- **Adaptive Tables**: Column labels are optimized (e.g., "PERCENT") to ensure no horizontal scrolling mess on small screens.
- **Blur-Teaser**: The `/demo` route provides a full-fidelity "Teaser" that looks exactly like the real app, but keeps data blurred to drive premium conversions.

## 🛠️ Dev Notes for Marketing
- **Stack**: React + Vite + Supabase (Security-First).
- **Architecture**: Modular Column System (Easy to add new data points).
- **Cache Management**: Hard-coded `APP_VERSION` ensures users always see the latest data updates without browser cache issues.
